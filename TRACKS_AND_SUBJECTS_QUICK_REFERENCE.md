## 📝 Important Notes

1. **Grade 11 & 12**: All subjects have Grade 11 and Grade 12 versions
2. **3-Term Trimester**: Subjects are distributed across 3 terms per year
3. **Core Subjects**: Mandatory for all students in each pathway
4. **Specialized Subjects**: Depend on chosen track
5. **Capstone Projects**: Required in all TVL specializations
6. **Total Units**: Varies by track (38-44 units)

## 🔄 Subject Codes Format

**Format**: `SUBJ##`

- **SUBJ** = Subject abbreviation (ENG, MATH, PHYS, etc.)
- **##** = Grade level (11 for Grade 11, 12 for Grade 12)

**Examples**:
- `ENG11` = English Grade 11
- `MATH12` = Mathematics Grade 12
- `PHYS11` = Physics Grade 11
