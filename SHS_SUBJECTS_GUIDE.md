## 📚 Overview

The Philippine Senior High School (SHS) curriculum is divided into two main pathways:

1. **Academic Pathway** - College preparation
2. **Technical-Professional (TechPro) Pathway** - Skills-based training

Each pathway has different tracks with specific subjects.

## 🎯 Subject Codes Explanation

**Format**: `SUBJ##`

- **SUBJ** = Subject abbreviation (ENG, MATH, PHYS, etc.)
- **##** = Grade level (11 for Grade 11, 12 for Grade 12)

**Examples**:
- `ENG 11` = English Grade 11
- `MATH 12` = Mathematics Grade 12
- `PHYS 11` = Physics Grade 11

---

## 📋 Grade 12 Subjects

Grade 12 subjects follow the same structure as Grade 11 but with advanced content:

- All Grade 11 subjects have Grade 12 equivalents
- Subject codes change from `##11` to `##12`
- More advanced and specialized content
- Preparation for college or career entry

**Example**:
- Grade 11: `PROG 11` (Programming 1)
- Grade 12: `PROG 12` (Programming 2 - Advanced)

## 📝 Notes

2. **Track/Specialization Subjects** depend on student's chosen track
3. **Units** represent credit hours per subject
4. **Electives** may be available depending on school offerings
5. **Capstone Projects** are required in all TVL specializations
