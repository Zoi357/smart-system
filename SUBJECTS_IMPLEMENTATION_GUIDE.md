## 📝 Implementation Notes

1. **Subject Codes**: Format is `SUBJ##` where ## is grade level (11 or 12)
2. **Units**: Credit hours per subject (typically 2-4 units)
3. **Max Capacity**: Maximum students per class (typically 25-50)
4. **Teacher Assignment**: Each subject is assigned to a teacher
5. **Prerequisites**: Some subjects require prior knowledge
6. **Electives**: Schools may offer additional electives

## 🎯 How to Use This Guide

1. **For Enrollment**: Students select subjects based on their pathway/track
2. **For Grade Tracking**: Teachers submit grades for each subject per term
3. **For Attendance**: Track attendance for each subject
4. **For Reporting**: Generate reports by subject, track, or pathway
